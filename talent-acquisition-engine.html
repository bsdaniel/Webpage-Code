What is our Talent Acquisition Engine?

Radixbay's Rural Talent Acquisition Engine is a method that has been proven to be a Scalable, and Efficient way to search for local talent for individuals who asipre careers in various IT professions. In participating in the Rural Talent Acquisition Engine program, individuals will undergo a set of behavioral based interviews, technical assessments, and immersive training hosted by our niche recruiters to bring out the most talent of our candidates. After the vetting process is finialized, individuals would have obtained a set of transferable technical skills that can be transformed and utilized as a RadixBay employee developing a level of work experience that will propel their professional careers.
